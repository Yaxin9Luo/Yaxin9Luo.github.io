🧑‍🎓I'm a final year undergraduate student at DTU Denmark.

😍 My research interests fall into the field of Multi-Modalities learning, such as Visual Language Grounding in images/videos, Video Understanding, Better Alignment for vision, language, and audio data. Specifically, I am super interested in training the intelligent model that combines egocentric and exocentric videos. In my opinion, this is the way to produce truly human-level AI, as well as highly useful assistant AI, to support daily human activities.

🧗 I am currently working on a research project in Efficient Large Multimodal Language Model using mixure of experts method.
